About Me

I am a software engineer at Glean. I began a doctoral program in Computer and Information Science at the University of Pennsylvania, where I was advised by Rajeev Alur, and eventually mastered out. During my time at Penn I worked on verification of neural network controllers for the F1/10 autonomous vehicle testbed.

Before Penn, I was an undergraduate at the department of Computer Science, Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay.

I love birding, cooking, baking, ice cream making, backpacking, and vibe chasing in no particular order.

In my free time, I've been building a better map of San Francisco's street trees.
